# minimize/ — fixture files

Each subfolder contains a pair of files:
- `input.dot`    : DFA to minimize
- `expected.dot` : DFA produced by `minimize(input)` — minimum number of states

`expected.dot` files are auto-generated by a temporary `GenerateMinimizeFixtures.java` utility.

## Subfolders

| Folder | Language                | Input states | Expected states | Notes |
|--------|-------------------------|:------------:|:---------------:|-------|
| `already_minimal/` | `a*` over `{a,b}`       | 2 | 2 | DFA is already minimal — no fusion happens |
| `two_equivalent_finals/` | `01+` over `{0,1}`      | 5 | 4 | States 2 and 3 are equivalent finals — fused into one |
| `dead_states/` | `(ab)*` over `{a,b}`    | 5 | 3 | Two distinct dead (trap) states — fused into one |
| `many_redundant/` | `a(a\|b)*` over `{a,b}` | 6 | 3 | Four equivalent final states — all fused into one |
| `single_state/` | `Σ*` over `{a,b}`       | 1 | 1 | Single all-accepting state — already minimal |
| `eight_states/` | `((01\|10)1*0)*(01\|10)1*` | 8 | 5 | Example from Dean Kelley's book |
| `two_equivalent_paths/` | `{0,1} ∪ {0,1}³{0,1}*`  | 6 | 4 | States 1 and 2 are equivalent — fused into one |
| `zero_oneplus/` | `01+` over `{0,1}` | 5 | 4 | Example from Francisco Bavera's class notes |
